With the explosion of the number of vehicles plying over the roads in the past few years, the problem of management of traffic in urban areas has become increasingly difficult. The existing vehicle management system and traffic fine collection system in India still depends heavily on human intervention and manual power to work. There have been few efforts at changing this system with the help of image recognition and license plate detection using pictures captured by traffic policemen on duty


Number of accidents does seem to decline but various factors come into play for the current data such as increased awareness, safer driving and covid pandemic greatly decreasing the amout of commuters on the Bangalore roads
